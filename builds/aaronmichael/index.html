About

I'm from Arnold California.(By Lake Tahoe) I loved growing up there because the forest was my playground, and there was always a new adventure around the corner, and beautiful scenery everywhere. One of the first things I remember growing up was my mom playing guitar for me. She would sing old folk songs to me when I was young. My mom also bought me my first guitar when I was five. I got in the school band when I was in the fifth grade and played the trumpet till high school. When I was 16 I picked up the electric guitar. I loved the electric guitar and the way it sounded. My favorite guitar player in high school was Jimi Hendrix, and I would sit and watch his videos over and over again until I learned his songs. I also started writing my own music in high school. I loved putting combinations of chords, and witty lyrics together in hopes that it would change the world. :) I also had a great guitar teacher named Dave Freedman. Dave grew up in Hawaii and played surf rock until he got a place in the band "Steppenwolf". I am currently taking Electronic Media, and Commercial Music Production, and voice classes at the University of Cincinnati College Conservatory of Music.

I really love performing and sharing my music with other people, its really one of the greatest feelings in the world. I currently play a solo acoustic guitar show, even though I hope to start a band when I have time.
